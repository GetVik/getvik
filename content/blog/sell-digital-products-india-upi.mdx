## The Challenge Indian Creators Face

If you're an Indian creator trying to sell digital products online, you've probably hit these roadblocks:

- **Stripe isn't available in India** — most global platforms rely on it
- **Card payment failures** — Indian cards often get declined on international sites
- **USD pricing confusion** — buyers don't want to calculate exchange rates
- **High platform fees** — global platforms charge 5-10% + currency conversion fees
- **Complicated payouts** — waiting weeks for international transfers, losing money to FX fees

These aren't small inconveniences. They're real barriers that stop talented creators from earning online.

## Payment Methods That Work in India

A good platform for selling digital products in India should support:

### Essential Payment Methods

1. **UPI** — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM
2. **Debit/Credit Cards** — Visa, Mastercard, RuPay
3. **Net Banking** — all major Indian banks
4. **Wallets** — Paytm, PhonePe wallet, etc.

Most Indian buyers prefer UPI for small to medium purchases (₹100-₹5,000) because it's instant and free.

For larger purchases, cards and net banking provide additional options.

## Common Questions About Selling Digital Products in India

### Do I need GST registration to sell digital products?

Not necessarily. GST registration is required only if your annual turnover exceeds ₹20 lakhs (₹10 lakhs for special category states). 

GetVik is currently not GST-registered. You're responsible for your own tax compliance. Keep records of your earnings for income tax filing.

### What about income tax on digital product sales?

Yes, income from digital product sales is taxable under the Income Tax Act. Maintain proper records of:
- Order reports from GetVik
- Payout statements
- Bank statements showing received payments

Consult a CA for personalized tax advice.

### Can I sell the same product on multiple platforms?

Yes! There's no exclusivity requirement. You can sell on GetVik, Gumroad, your own website, etc. simultaneously.

### What if a buyer asks for a refund?

Digital products are non-returnable by nature. GetVik does not support refunds for digital products. 

**Best practice**: Write clear, accurate product descriptions to set proper expectations.

### How do I handle customer support?

Buyers can contact you through GetVik's messaging system. You're responsible for:
- Answering product questions
- Providing download support
- Handling any product-related issues

### Can I sell courses or video content?

Yes, but as **downloadable files** only. GetVik supports one-time digital downloads. 

Not currently supported:
- Subscriptions
- Memberships
- Streaming video platforms
- Recurring billing

## Tips for Success Selling Digital Products in India

### 1. Solve a Real Problem

Don't create products just because you can. Identify pain points:
- What do people in your niche struggle with?
- What questions do they ask repeatedly?
- What would save them time or money?

### 2. Build in Public

Share your creation process on Twitter/LinkedIn:
- "Building a Notion template for freelancers..."
- "Day 3 of creating my productivity system..."
- "Almost done with my Figma design pack..."

This builds anticipation and an audience before launch.

### 3. Price for Your Audience

Indian buyers are price-sensitive but willing to pay for quality:
- Start with competitive pricing
- Offer launch discounts (20-30% off)
- Test different price points
- Don't undervalue your work

### 4. Create High-Quality Previews

- Show screenshots of your product
- Create demo videos (even simple screen recordings)
- Write detailed descriptions
- List exactly what's included

### 5. Leverage Indian Social Media

- **Twitter/X**: Tech and creator community
- **LinkedIn**: Professional tools and courses
- **Instagram**: Design and lifestyle products
- **WhatsApp**: Share with existing communities
